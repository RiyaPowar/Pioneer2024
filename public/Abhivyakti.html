ABHIVYAKTI:

Paper Presentation Competition

Prizes:
1st: ₹ 10000
2nd: ₹ 7500
3rd: ₹ 5000

Registration Fee: ₹ 50 per group (Abstract).
₹ 100 per participant (Once the team is shortlisted).


Theme:


1. Internet of Things: Revolutionizing connectivity and transforming industries.
2. Zero Carbon Systems.
3. Use of Nanomaterials in Construction.
4. Business Intelligence and Data Visualization.
5. Industry 5.0.
6. Deep Learning Applications Transforming Computer Vision: Current Trends and Future Prospects.
7. Sustainable Development.
8. Renewable Energy.
9. Recent advancements and development in Electric Vehicle Technology.


General Rules and Guidelines:


1. The paper must be in IEEE format and must have multidisciplinary technical content.
2. Paper should be sent to kitabhivyakti@gmail.com (subject must be in the following format - participantname_collegename_topicname).
3. Deadline for submission(abstract) is 15th February 2024, accetance shall be communicated before 18th February 2024 (via mail/whatsapp).
4. Token amount of Rs. 50/- per paper (per group) shall be submitted while registration (which is non-refundable) & Rs. 100/- per Participant must be paid after the paper is shortlisted.
5. Maximum 2 participants are allowed per group.






About Us

PIONEER, a National-level technical event organised by KITCoEK under ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education). The Pioneer, in its 27th edition, stands as the flagship event of KITCoEK with a rich legacy spanning over 26 years. Two central events are Abhivyati and Prakalp. Along with two central events, Pioneer features nine departmental events, each meticulously organized by different departmental student associations.
