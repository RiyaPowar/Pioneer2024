About Us

PIONEER, a National-level technical event organised by KITCoEK under ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education). The Pioneer, in its 27th edition, stands as the flagship event of KITCoEK with a rich legacy spanning over 26 years. Two central events are Abhivyati and Prakalp. Along with two central events, Pioneer features nine departmental events, each meticulously organized by different departmental student associations.
